How to play

  • Cover the board with four words.
  • Each word uses four or five edge-connected hexes.
  • The pale hexes are holes and are not used.
  • Click hexes one by one, or drag across them.
  • Four-letter words are accepted after a short pause or by pressing Enter.
  • Tap a completed word, then tap its x to remove it.
  • Reading order: it's complicated.

Penthex Generation Order Review

Each card is one fixed penthex orientation, numbered to match the generated shape list. Single-path penthexes, shapes with a unique right/down path, shapes with a unique two-move-type path, shapes with a unique horizontal/down legal path, two-legal-path shapes with a rightward first-step preference, and shapes with a unique always-rightward path, and height-four shapes are omitted here because they have an unambiguous standard order. The cell labels are raw shape indices; the top-right sequence is my first-pass guess at natural generation order. Shape coordinates are shown below each tile for cross-checking.

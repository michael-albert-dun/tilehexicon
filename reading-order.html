Reading order

Unlike English text, or even the squares in a Tilexicon puzzle, it's hard to find a natural reading order for the hexes in a polyhex. The decision made in Tilehexicon is to generate tiles in orders that seem to read fairly naturally, but to allow the solver to choose any set of tiles that form a valid word. As a result, their may be alternate solutions to a given puzzle that use the different words and/or different tiles. If you find such a solution, well done.

The orders used for generation were chosen after a deep and complete scientific analysis of the various possibilities, by a pool of highly qualified and experienced puzzle designers (N = 1). After various heuristics were applied to the possible orders, there were still a significant number of ambiguous cases, and the final decision was made by vibes.
